Our story

A family workshop, run on craft and trust

Amar Furniture began as a small wood workshop in Nashik and grew, one satisfied home at a time, into a full-service furniture maker.

Run as a family business alongside skilled local karigars, we've spent over two decades doing one thing well — turning good wood into furniture that families keep for years.

We handle everything under one roof: design, joinery, lathe work, finishing and installation. That control is why our pieces feel solid, fit right, and last.
